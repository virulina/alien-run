pureya

    A downloadable game for Windows, macOS, Linux, and Android

    Pureya is a collection of arcade minigames that play with just 2 buttons.
    Every 10 seconds, the game will jump from one minigame to the next.

    Avoid obstacles and collect marbles, then use those marbles at a pachinko machine to unlock skins and new minigames.

    The current version is a beta, pureya is still in development.
